## About

**Patabuga Enterprise** is an Independent Research Institute & Applied Technology Lab dedicated to advanced system development within the scientific and technical sectors. We explore the frontiers of digital infrastructure through three strategic pillars: **Innovative**, **Transformative**, and **Extractive**.

### Strategic Pillars

| Pillar | Focus |
|--------|-------|
| 🔬 **Innovative** | Research and development of novel system architectures |
| 🔄 **Transformative** | Digital transformation through automation and AI integration |
| 🏗️ **Extractive** | Building resilient, sovereign digital infrastructure |
